    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Weather Chef?

    Weather Chef is a website that provides weather forecasts and cooking recommendations based on the current weather conditions.

    How do I use Weather Chef?

    To use Weather Chef, simply enter your location or allow the website to access your location automatically. Then, you'll receive weather forecasts along with suggested recipes tailored to the current weather.

    Is Weather Chef free to use?

    Yes, Weather Chef is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges.

    Can I customize the recipes?

    Unfortunately, recipe customization is not available at the moment. However, we're constantly working to improve our services, and this feature may be added in the future.
